**I want to prototype a dashboard with the following:\
\
Problem**

Pilates instructors currently juggle multiple disconnected tools ---
spreadsheets, notes apps, and paper planners --- to schedule classes,
design exercise progressions, track clients, and manage their teaching
library. This fragmented workflow makes it hard to stay organized, reuse
class content, track client progress, and scale their practice over
time.

**Solution**

Build a centralized SaaS platform purpose-built for Pilates instructors
that unifies class scheduling, structured class planning with layered
exercise progressions, a rich exercise library, and client session
tracking --- giving instructors everything they need to plan, teach, and
grow in one place.

**MVP Scope**

-   Users --- authenticated accounts (instructor-centric for MVP)

-   Classes --- scheduled instances (calendar events)

-   Class Plan Templates (reusable, decoupled from schedule)

-   Exercise Library (with folders, tags, descriptions, cueing,
    regressions/progressions, image upload)

-   Exercise Layers / Progressions (parent → child relational structure)

-   Clients & session notes --- profiles, notes, injuries/focus/goals,
    exercises used, timeline per client

-   Basic weekly teaching stats

**Features**

**Calendar & Class Planning**

-   **Calendar Dashboard** --- Weekly/monthly view of all scheduled
    classes with at-a-glance status

-   **Class Planner** --- Structured builder for designing class plans
    with ordered exercise sequences

-   **Class Plan Templates** --- Reusable class structures that can be
    scheduled independently without duplicating content

-   **Layered Exercise Progressions** --- Modular parent → progression
    level system for building adaptive exercise flows

**Exercise Library**

-   **Create & Save Exercises** --- Build a personal library of
    exercises with rich metadata

-   **Equipment-Based Folders** --- Categorize exercises by apparatus or
    equipment type

-   **Exercise Detail Storage** --- Per-exercise fields for description,
    regressions/progressions, cueing ideas, and image upload

-   **Load into Class Plans** --- Directly pull library exercises into
    any class plan or template

-   **Search & Filter** --- Find exercises quickly by name, tag, folder,
    or equipment

**Client Notes**

-   **Client Profiles** --- Create and manage individual client records

-   **Session Notes** --- Log notes after each session tied to a
    specific class or date

-   **Health & Goals Tracking** --- Store injuries, focus areas, and
    goals per client

-   **Exercise Attachments** --- Link exercises used in a session to the
    client\'s record

-   **Timeline View** --- Chronological history of all sessions and
    notes per client

**Reporting**

-   **Basic Teaching Stats** --- Weekly overview of classes taught,
    exercise variety, and plan reuse

**Out of Scope (Future Phases)**

-   Video storage for exercises

-   Team / multi-instructor functionality

-   Client-facing portal

-   Billing and subscription management


**User Roles**

-   **Instructor (Solo)** --- Default MVP role. Full access to their own
    classes, templates, library, and clients.

-   **Admin** *(Future)* --- Platform-level management for studio/team
    accounts.

-   **Team Member** *(Future)* --- Secondary instructors under a studio
    account with scoped access.
